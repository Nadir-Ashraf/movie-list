My Faviorate Movies

1. Manchester By Sea (2016)

Manchester by the Sea is not a doctor-prescribed antidote to cure depression or grief. However, it is one of the perfect films to teach a lesson about how to cope.

2. Poject X (2012)

Three high school seniors decide to throw a party in order to gain popularity among their peers. However, their plan soon becomes unmanageable when news of their gathering quickly gets out.

3. Forrest Gump (1994)

Forrest, a man with low IQ, recounts the early years of his life when he found himself in the middle of key historical events. All he wants now is to be reunited with his childhood sweetheart, Jenny.

4. The Hangover (2009)

A few days before his wedding, Doug Billings and his best men go to Las Vegas for a bachelor party. However, the next day, the friends realise that they have no recollection of the previous night.

5. Nightcrawler (2014)

ou Bloom, a petty thief, becomes a photojournalist and sells footage of sensational and violent stories in Los Angeles to Nina, a news director. Soon, Lou turns desperate to stay ahead of the game.

6. Into the Wild (2007)

Christopher McCandless, a top student and athlete, donates his savings and hitch-hikes to Alaska to live in the wilderness. Along the way, he faces numerous challenges and meets interesting people.
